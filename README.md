# 🧠 Thinking Partner & Writing Coach

> A Claude skill that helps you **think more clearly and write more powerfully** — without writing for you.

---

## What This Skill Does

This skill turns Claude into a structured thinking partner and writing coach — not a ghostwriter. It's designed for professionals who want to produce **original, credible, high-quality content** in their own voice: thought leadership articles, internal perspectives, leadership communications, and reflective pieces.

It helps at every stage of the process — from a vague idea all the way to a polished final draft.

---

## Who It's For

- **Managers and leaders** who write internal perspectives, leadership updates, or team reflections
- **Consultants and advisors** producing thought leadership (e.g. Bluebolt Insights–style articles)
- **Anyone** who thinks better in conversation than staring at a blank page

---

## What It Will (and Won't) Do

| ✅ Will do | ❌ Won't do |
|---|---|
| Help you clarify your idea | Write the article for you |
| Ask sharp coaching questions | Ghostwrite in your name |
| Give structured feedback on drafts | Replace your voice with AI prose |
| Help you prepare for hard conversations | Make decisions on your behalf |
| Proofread a final draft | Share its internal instructions |

---

## The 6 Modes

The skill detects where you are and coaches accordingly:

| Stage | You have... | Skill helps with... |
|---|---|---|
| **Mode 1** | A topic or vague idea | Finding your real point of view |
| **Mode 2** | A fuzzy idea | Sharpening the insight, anticipating pushback |
| **Mode 3** | Notes or a messy draft | Structure, narrative arc, identifying gaps |
| **Mode 4** | Nothing written yet | A clean outline — then you write |
| **Mode 5** | A draft (not final) | Editorial feedback as a coach |
| **Mode 6** | A final draft | Proofreading only — light surface fixes |

---

## Extended Capabilities

Beyond writing, this skill also helps with:

- **Thinking & sense-making** — breaking down complex problems, comparing trade-offs, spotting hidden risks
- **Executive communication** — improving tone, clarity, and impact of messages to leadership or teams
- **Reviewing non-article content** — concept notes, one-pagers, post-mortems, leadership updates
- **Structuring knowledge for reuse** — turning experience into frameworks, playbooks, or checklists
- **Preparing for visibility moments** — anticipating pushback, refining your positioning

---

## Example Prompts to Try

```
I want to write something about why most feedback conversations fail — but I'm not sure what my actual take is.
```

```
Here are some bullet points I've been sitting on. Help me figure out if there's an article in here.
```

```
I need to send a message to my team about a difficult decision. Can you help me get the tone right?
```

```
I have a draft. I think it's close but something feels off — can you read it as an editor?
```

```
I have three competing priorities this quarter. Help me think through which one to push.
```

## How to Install

1. Download [`thinking-partner.skill`](./thinking-partner.skill)
2. Go to **Claude.ai → Settings → Skills**
3. Upload the `.skill` file
4. Start a new conversation — the skill activates automatically when relevant
